User Knowledge Breakdown

Understanding the spectrum of AI awareness and comprehension

Knowledge Segmentation

Clueless Users (85-95%)

These users interact with AI tools daily but have little to no understanding of how they work. They may know about interfaces like ChatGPT but are unaware of concepts like neural networks, training data, or tokenization.

Power Users (<1%)

A small elite group with deep technical understanding of AI systems. They comprehend transformer architectures, can fine-tune models, and understand the mathematical foundations of machine learning.

Basic Awareness (10-20%)

Users with surface-level understanding. They know AI can generate text and images, understand basic limitations, but lack deeper technical knowledge. They can effectively use prompts but don't understand the underlying mechanisms.

Oblivious Masses

Users who are either unaware of AI capabilities or completely dismiss them. They may believe AI is just "fancy autocomplete" or fear it as a job-stealing threat without understanding its actual applications.
